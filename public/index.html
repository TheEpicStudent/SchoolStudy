School Studies

Music and sounds to help you focus and study

Open Beats

Beats is an app that plays music that is more focused on more relaxing genres, so you can focus more.

Disclaimer: This does not include lofi, as we are making a dedicated lofi app.

Open Noise Generator (Alpha)

Noise Generator is an app that plays sounds that help you focus, like rain, white noise, and more.

Disclaimer: This is in alpha, and might not work as expected.

Open the Pomodoro Timer

A timer that switches between a "Focus" timer and a "Break" timer. You can learn how you can use it efficiently on the page.

Open the Notes App

This is a simple notes app where you can jot down your thoughts and ideas while studying. It helps you stay organized and focused.
